How Do I Reach Space?

I have always found intrest in the sky. Even as a child I would look up and wonder what was out there? How can I get there? Well, space is not easily reached so I found pictures and words to help me achieve space travel. I didn't ever think it was going to be achievable to get to outer space, but now I just need to save up for a trip to space. Thanks to Virgin Galactics ambition I am one step closer, but that comes with a price. A very hefty one. I didn't let that get me down though! I just thought if I could get 200k people to donate a dollar to help out I could float that kind of bill.

This site I created is dedicated to helping me raise the funds to achieve my lifelong dream of checking out what space is like. It has become a social experiment to see if people I dont even know will help out and get me a ride to outer space. I came up with the idea from living in Minneapolis and watching video's on Youtube. When I lived in the Twin Citys area people would ask for spare change to ride the bus or whatever. It usually was outside Chicago Lake Liquor so it wasn't very hard to figure out what your spare change was going towards. I figured I would take an honest approach if I'm going to pan-handle my way to space. Also watching viral video's on Youtube that have had millions of viewers/hits gave me the idea to expand this idea to the web. Because I don't really have time to stand on the corner somewhere and ask people for spare change.
